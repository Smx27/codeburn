# Acceptable Use Policy

Acceptable use policy for AIInsight.

**Last Updated:** June 20, 2026

## Overview

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") describes acceptable use of AIInsight. By using our service, you agree to comply with this policy.

## Permitted Uses

### Primary Use Cases

- Track AI coding token usage
- Analyze AI coding costs
- Monitor team AI usage
- Optimize AI spending
- Generate usage reports

### Acceptable Activities

- Sync usage data from your machines
- View analytics on your dashboard
- Share data with your team
- Export data for analysis
- Integrate with your workflows

## Prohibited Uses

### Illegal Activities

- Violate any applicable laws or regulations
- Infringe on intellectual property rights
- Transmit harmful or malicious code
- Attempt to access unauthorized data
- Engage in fraud or deception

### Harmful Activities

- Disrupt or undermine the service
- Interfere with other users
- Distribute malware or viruses
- Conduct denial-of-service attacks
- Scrape or harvest user data

### Commercial Activities

- Resell or redistribute the service
- Use for competitive analysis
- Build competing products
- Unauthorized commercial use

### Content Violations

- Store or transmit illegal content
- Violate third-party rights
- Transmit spam or unsolicited messages
- Promote hate speech or violence
- Exploit minors

## Data Usage

### What You Can Do

- Sync your own usage data
- View your own analytics
- Export your own data
- Share with your team

### What You Cannot Do

- Access others' data
- Scrape user data
- Aggregate data without consent
- Sell user data
- Use data for unauthorized purposes

## Technical Restrictions

### System Integrity

- Do not attempt to bypass security
- Do not exploit vulnerabilities
- Do not interfere with infrastructure
- Do not modify the service

### Resource Usage

- Do not exceed rate limits
- Do not abuse API endpoints
- Do not consume excessive resources
- Do not disrupt service availability

## Enforcement

### Monitoring

We may monitor usage to:
- Detect policy violations
- Ensure service integrity
- Protect user security
- Comply with legal requirements

### Actions

We may take action if we detect violations:
- Warning notification
- Temporary suspension
- Permanent termination
- Legal action

### Appeals

If you believe action was taken in error:
- Contact support@aiinsight.dev
- Provide explanation and evidence
- We will review within 5 business days

## Reporting Violations

If you become aware of violations:
- Email: abuse@aiinsight.dev
- GitHub: Report on repository
- Include evidence and details

## Consequences

### Minor Violations

- Warning email
- Request to stop
- Monitoring increased

### Serious Violations

- Temporary suspension
- Account restriction
- Data preservation

### Severe Violations

- Permanent termination
- Legal action
- Law enforcement referral
